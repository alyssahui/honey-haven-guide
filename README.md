## Gameplay Features 

Press the play button to navigate to the daylist page. Explore the days as Marty R., the mailmouse of a quaint, mysterious island called Honey Haven. Talk to townsfolk, deliver the mail, play minigames, and get absorbed in a vibrant story!


When playing the visual novel parts:

- Click the screen or right arrow key to move forward in the story after the text is said.
  
- To fast forward through text, click the screen or right arrow key once. The text will become visible at once.

- Clicking the left arrow key or back button in the top left corner of the screen will bring you to the previous text.
  

When playing the Carrot Delivery minigame:

- Shoot carrots into mailboxes! Avoid anyone or anything who may be on the road...

- Use w to move up and s to move down.
  
- Use the up arrow key to shoot carrots upward and down arrow key to shoot downwards.


When playing the Chester Chase minigame:

- Avoid getting caught by Chester!

- Use the arrow keys to move in their directions.

- Hide behind rocks but be careful... Chester can jump over them!

- Avoid getting caught in mousetraps.

- Use portals to teleport.

- Get cheese for a speed boost!
